Welcome to the Garage Sale Marketplace API!

This API powers a community-driven marketplace for garage sale items—connecting people who want to give away or find second-hand goods in their region.

Getting Started

  • Auth0 authentication is required to use the API.
  • You need a Phantom Wallet with at least 0.004 SOL (as of today) to post listings, bid, pause, or access seller contact info (Telegram or phone).

Allowed Listings

You can list only garage sale items that do not require special permissions:

  • Old textbooks and books
  • Clothes and shoes
  • Bikes, toys, gaming consoles, video games, DVDs
  • Electronics and other used household items

Not allowed: Illegal items, food, drugs, or anything requiring special permissions.

Listing Types

  • Looking for – Post if you are searching for something.
  • Wanting to get rid of – Post if you want to give away or sell something you own.

Marketplace Features

  • Search and sort listings by region or keyword.
  • Set a price in any currency (optional)—the main goal is matchmaking, not profit.
  • Contact sellers via Telegram or phone (requires wallet and fee).
  • Pause or bid on listings (requires wallet and fee).

Fees & Support

Each listing or connection costs 0.004 SOL to help finance the project.
